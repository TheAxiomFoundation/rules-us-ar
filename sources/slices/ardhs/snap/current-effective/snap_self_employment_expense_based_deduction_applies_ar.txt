Costs of Producing Self-Employment Income:

Costs of producing self-employment income are excluded from the gross income.
Allowable costs include, but are not limited to, the identifiable costs of:
labor, stock, raw material and supplies, licensing fees, business
transportation costs, rental payments on income producing property, and
